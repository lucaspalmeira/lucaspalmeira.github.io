ABOUT

I am an enthusiast of Structural Bioinformatics and Machine Learning (AI). Graduated with a degree in Biological Sciences Education from UESB, I am currently pursuing a master's degree in Bioinformatics at PPGBIOINFO - UFMG, and I am a member of the Laboratory of Bioinformatics and Computational Chemistry (LBQC-UESB).

As a member of LQBC-UESB, I am responsible for assisting in projects ranging from molecule screening to the use of artificial intelligence for predicting their activity. During my undergraduate studies, specifically at LQBC-UESB, I was a grantee of both scientific initiation (Fapesb) and technological initiation (CNPq) under the guidance of Professor Dr. Bruno Silva Andrade. In my first year, I worked on a project involving the screening of natural compounds for the inhibition of key enzymes in the metabolism of Leishmania spp. In the other two years as a grantee, I was involved in a project to develop a pipeline for modeling and docking of peptides. In the same project, I was also responsible for collaborating on the construction of software to predict the bioactivity of peptides against the Spike Glycoprotein of SARS-CoV-2 (Covid-19). Recently, I started my Master's in Bioinformatics at the Graduate Program in Bioinformatics at the Federal University of Minas Gerais (PPGBIOINFO-UFMG).
